    • About Us

    Welcome to Shelterhub, the premier rental app for students in Kota. Our mission is to simplify the process of finding accommodations and mess facilities, connecting students with their ideal PGs, hostels, and messes while helping property owners find suitable tenants effortlessly. With an extensive database of options, our app provides detailed information about each listing, including facilities, room types, pricing, and proximity to educational institutions. High-resolution images give you a clear idea of the property before making a decision. Refine your search with advanced filters and read genuine reviews from previous students to get insights into the quality of services. Join Shelterhub today for the easiest way to find and rent accommodations and mess facilities in Kota.

    How We Work!

    User App

    Our app is designed with students in mind, offering an intuitive and user-friendly interface to help them explore various options for their accommodation needs. Whether you're looking for a cozy PG, a vibrant hostel, or a reliable mess, our app provides detailed listings with essential information, including location, facilities, rental rates, and photos. With advanced search and filtering options, students can narrow down their preferences and find the perfect fit within their budget and requirements.

    Owner App

    We understand the challenges property owners face in reaching the right tenants for their PGs, hostels, and mess facilities. Our app provides a hassle-free platform for property owners to list their properties and attract the right audience. By showcasing your property on our app, you can significantly reduce the time and effort spent searching for potential occupants. Our extensive reach and targeted user base ensure that your property receives maximum visibility, resulting in a streamlined process of finding suitable tenants.
