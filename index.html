    • About Us

    Welcome to Shelterhub, the premier rental app designed to simplify the process of finding accommodations and mess facilities for students in Kota. Our mission is to provide a seamless platform that connects students with their ideal PGs, hostels, and messes while helping property owners effortlessly find suitable tenants.

    We have curated an extensive database of PGs, hostels, and mess facilities in Kota, ensuring that you have access to a wide range of options. Each listing on our app provides comprehensive details about the accommodation, including facilities, room types, pricing, proximity to educational institutions and high-resolution images are also available to give you a clear idea of the property before making a decision.

    Our app offers advanced search filters, allowing you to refine your search based on your specific preferences. And our app also features genuine reviews and ratings from students who have previously stayed at the listed accommodations. This way, you can get insights into the quality of services and overall satisfaction levels.

    Whether you're a student in search of the perfect accommodation or a property owner looking for the right tenants, Shelterhub is here to make the process seamless. Join us today and experience the easiest way to find and rent accommodations and mess facilities in Kota.
